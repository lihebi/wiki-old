---
title: job control
---

* C-Z suspend
* refer a job
  - `%n`: job number
  - `%%`: current job
  - `%+`: current job
  - `%-`: previous job
  - `%`: current job
  - `%ce`: the job "ce"
  - `%?ce`: the job, whose command has "ce"

* fg %1: continue it in foreground
* bg %1: continue it in background
* jobs: list jobs
* kill %1: kill the job
